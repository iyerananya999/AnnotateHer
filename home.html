How it works

01 Upload your text

Paste or upload any historical writing, textbook chapter, or scientific article as a .txt or .md file.

02 Detection runs

AnnotateHer scans each paragraph for men credited with discoveries, then checks whether their female collaborators are named anywhere in the text.

03 The graph appears

A live knowledge graph surfaces beside each flagged paragraph — showing the credited man, the concept, and the woman who was written out.
